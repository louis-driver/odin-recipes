Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a nice hearty meal that pairs woderfully with you bread of choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs hamburger meat
  • 40 oz tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 8 oz tomato paste
  • 1 pint cottage chees
  • 1 lb. cream cheese
  • 1 pint of ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 oz lasagna noodle sheets
  • Your preferred garlic bread (not optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil
  3. Add lasagna noodle to water to cook for the designated time, then drain and set aside
  4. Get a large pan on the stove set to medium heat
  5. Cook meat until done, be sure to season well
  6. Drain meat of excess oil
  7. Combine cooked meat with tomato products to allow flavors to blend
  8. Combine all cheeses together, leaving 1/2 cup of mozzarella for topping
  9. In a baking dish, layer the meat and sauce mixture, then cheese mixture, then noodles until fully used
  10. Top with remaining mozzarella and bake for 25 minutes or until the mozzarella browns some
  11. Bake your garlic bread
  12. And finally, enjoy!
