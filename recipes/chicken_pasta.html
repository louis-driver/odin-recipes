Chicken Pasta

Description

This chicken pasta is a quick and easy meal that is light and refreshing for those hotter days.

Ingredients

  • 20 oz of rotini pasta (or another short pasta)
  • 1 lb chicken of choice (to be shredded so it doesn't much matter)
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 cup sweet peppers
  • Olive oil
  • Your Prefered Seasoning

Steps

  1. Set a large pot of water on the stove to boil
  2. Add pasta to boiling water according to the box's instructions
  3. Cook chicken in oil, be sure to season well
  4. Shred the chicken
  5. Cut onion and peppers into thin slivers
  6. Cook sliced vegetables until the onion is translucent
  7. Drain the pasta and all your ingredients to the pot with additional olive oil
  9. And finally, enjoy!
