Ugly Dip

Description

Ugly dip is a great meal for those colder nights when you want something cooked up quick. Its warmth just puts the soul at ease

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of black beans
  • 2 cans of kidney beans
  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes with green chiles
  • 2 cans of corn
  • 1 lb. of sausage or hamburger meat
  • 1 cup of velveta cheese
  • Taco and Ranch Seasoning
  • Tortilla chips or carb of choice

Steps

  1. Get a large pot on the stove set to medium heat
  2. Cook meat of choice until done, be sure to season well
  3. Drain meat of excess oil
  4. Add the remaining ingredients to the pot and heat through
  5. And finally, enjoy!
